4. Conclusion

So it is actually not easy to cultivate salmon, but the positive thing is that many technologies have been developed to cultivate salmon. Cultivation is also not always positive, the cons is that production costs are too expensive. My assumptions, an easy solution to keep the population of salmon still sustainable is to limit the catch by weight or by fishing season, and improve the technologies for Aquaculture to make the cost is cheaper.
